Alumni and Friends of AEPi Mu Chapter,


As the recently elected Master of the Alpha Epsilon Fraternity at UVA, I am very excited for this upcoming year. We currently have 40 active brothers and 15 pledges that I look forward to being able to call brothers. We are hard at work this semester putting together a great social calendar as well as putting the finishing touches on our spring philanthropy, our annual Food Fest. Our brotherhood has worked very hard this past semester in the classroom and I am pleased to say that last semester we had the highest GPA among all fraternities and sororities. With our brothers remaining active in both the Jewish community and our UVA community, we are in position for an amazing year and I look forward to seeing it unfold.


Zachary Massey

President, AEPi Mu Chapter
