Alumni and Friends of AEPi Mu Chapter,


As a rising fourth year and midway through my tenure as Master of the Mu Chapter, I am unbelievably excited for the upcoming semester. While we will greatly miss the departure of the Eta pledge class, our brotherhood is excited to return to a more normal era, strengthened mightily by the addition of the Kappa pledge class. Our brotherhood worked hard to maintain philanthropic and social ties throughout the COVID era, and we are excited to continue our growth into the future with eager anticipation. With brothers maintaining academic, extracurricular, and Jewish community related standards, we are poised for a continuing transformation in the upcoming semester. I can speak for us all when I say that we anticipate an extraordinary future for the Mu Chapter that sees us continuing to build ties within our school community, and I could not be prouder to be alongside my brothers in this journey.


Jack Fechter

President, AEPi Mu Chapter
