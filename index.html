At the University of Virginia

Alumni, Brothers and Friends,


My name is Ethan Schwartz, and I am in the third month of my term as Brother Master of the Mu Chapter of AEPi at UVA. At the moment, there is much to look back on, to celebrate, and equally to look forward to in the coming months. The chapter underwent a very successful rush cycle, recruiting a bright, sociable, and brotherly class of 17 new members earlier this year. The new and existing brothers continue to demonstrate the core values of our fraternity, engaging in philanthropy, donating their time to community service, and excelling academically. For the second semester in a row, our chapter has the highest GPA amongst all chapters in the Inter Fraternity Council at UVA. Many brothers continue to volunteer at the University Hospital, Madison House, and local schools in the Charlottesville community, and we look forward to running another successful philanthropic campaign for the UVA Heart Family Fund later this spring. Brothers also continue to engage with the Jewish community at UVA, with many holding leadership positions at the Brody Jewish Center. Finally, this year marks 100 years since the founding of our chapter at UVA. We are proud of how far our fraternity has come over the years and are excited for a promising and bright future for the chapter.


Ethan Schwartz

Brother Master, Alpha Epsilon Pi Mu Chapter
