My name is Zach Garfinkel, and I am serving as the Rush Chair for the AEPi Mu Chapter this upcoming year. After this past year where rush was limited by the challenges of the pandemic, we are all extremely excited to hopefully have a normal rush. Rush is such a fun process for those rushing and for us, so everyone should be looking forward to January 2022. As school starts back up in the fall, our rush committee will get together and begin planning our rush events, which are always highlights of the year. We're looking forward to meeting the new UVA students and getting started!
